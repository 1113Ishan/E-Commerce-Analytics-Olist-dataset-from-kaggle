1. E-commerce Analytics Platform

This project shows that you can turn raw operational data into business insights.

The Business Problem

An online store has data in multiple tables, but leadership cannot easily answer questions like:

Which products generate the most revenue?

Are customers returning?

Which categories are growing or declining?

What is the average order value?

Which months drive the most sales?

Your job is to turn transactional data into a decision-making dashboard.

SQL Role

SQL is where the real analysis happens.

Examples of queries you will build:

Revenue per month

SELECT
DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) AS month,
SUM(payment_amount) AS revenue
FROM payments
JOIN orders USING(order_id)
GROUP BY month
ORDER BY month;

Top products

SELECT
product_name,
SUM(quantity) AS units_sold
FROM order_items
JOIN products USING(product_id)
GROUP BY product_name
ORDER BY units_sold DESC;

Customer lifetime value

SELECT
customer_id,
SUM(payment_amount) AS lifetime_value
FROM payments
JOIN orders USING(order_id)
GROUP BY customer_id;

These queries create the analytical dataset for BI tools.
